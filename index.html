Who Am I?

Devoted To Developing Quality Software Solutions

Hey there, thanks for stopping by. Having grown up in picturesque Richmond, North Yorkshire I have an inherent love for the countryside and Norman Castles. At the moment, I am residing in Camberwell, South East London. Up until the 19th Century, Camberwell was visited by Londoners for the healing properties of its mineral springs. Yet, I am here for the globe-spanning cuisine and overpriced housing.

My journey into software has been both fulfilling and liberating. Prior to developing, I spent 10 years in the music industry as a recording engineer, working with a whole host of clients to produce music, as well as audiobooks and podcasts. Nowadays, having spent formative time on the QA Software Development Bootcamp, you can find me honing my skills in a diverse range of technologies including but not limited to Java, Springboot, Maven, Python, SQL and Microsoft Azure. Building cleanly written software for both business and leisure is my passion and I will always strive to deliver the best results whatever the brief may require.
